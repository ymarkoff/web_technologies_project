Intro

The setting of Alter World allows for a huge diversity of classes. The story begins with two base classes and more will be added with new story Arcs.

Aberrants

Aberrants are people born with psychic powers or who have developed them through a special process. Their minds are able to bend the rules of physics and even reality to gain superpowers like Fire Manipulation, Telepathy and Telekinesis. Every Aberrant is limited to a Single Ability.

Examples

Fire Manipulation

This ability revolves around generation of fire as well as manipulating the form of existing flames.

  • Fire Blast
  • Ring of Fire
  • Extinguish

Telekinesis

This ability revolves around generation of an invisible force which can be used to interract with the surroundings of a person.

  • Attract
  • Shield
  • Flight

Teleportation

This ability revolves around generation of an invisible force which can be used to interract with the surroundings of a person.

  • Blink
  • Teleport Object
  • Spatial Swap

Magicians

Magicians are people born with no innate special abilities but who have learned to harness mystical forces by performing rituals and spells linked to the power of supernatural deities. Examples

Fairy Magic

This is a rare magical school, only accessible to people who have made a contract with the Fairy folk. The spells and rituals of this school revolve around manipulation of the senses.

  • Forgetfulness
  • Cloak of Majesty
  • Charm Person

Tao Magic

A magical school is based on the principles of Taoism. The spells and rituals of this school focus on divination, protection and communion with the dead.

  • Cleanse Illness
  • Spirit Barrier
  • Speak With The Dead

Alchemy

A magical practice focused on transmutation of various materials and creation of elixirs with supernatural qualities.

  • Transmute Metal
  • Firework
  • Create Elixir: Iron Skin
